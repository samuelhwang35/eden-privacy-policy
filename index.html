Eden Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Overview

Eden is a Bible devotional and journaling app designed to help you grow in faith through daily scripture reading, reflection, and prayer. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

Eden collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Devotional Entries: Your personal reflections, prayers, and notes
  • Reading Progress: Scripture passages you've read and completion dates
  • App Preferences: Bible translation selection and other app settings

How We Use Your Information

All information collected by Eden is used solely to:

  • Provide the devotional experience and track your spiritual growth
  • Sync your data across your Apple devices via Apple CloudKit
  • Maintain your app preferences and reading history

Data Storage and Synchronization

Your data is stored:

  • Locally: On your device using Core Data
  • Cloud Sync: Via Apple CloudKit to sync across your devices signed in with the same Apple ID
  • Scripture Content: Provided by API.Bible in accordance with their Fair Use Management System (FUMS)

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your devotional entries and personal data remain private to you.

Third-Party Services

Eden uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple CloudKit: For syncing your data across devices (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • API.Bible: For scripture content (governed by their privacy policy and FUMS compliance)

Data Security

Your data security is protected by:

  • Apple's CloudKit encryption and security measures
  • Local device security (Face ID, Touch ID, device passcode)
  • HTTPS encryption for all network communications

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your data by removing the app or using the reset function
  • Control CloudKit sync through your Apple ID settings

Children's Privacy

Eden does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences interested in Bible study and devotional practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
